Connecting

  • Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

    • I have tried using the trust technique with my manager at work. Keeping my manager informed of when I need time off work for this course and fitting me in for part time work when needed. Due to this I have managed to build up trust with my manager at work and his feelings have come across supportive in what I endeavour to do. So far everything has gone well.

  • Try empathic listening - how did it go?

    • I had a conversation with a family member, regarding a situation from late childhood. I could tell that this situation had been sitting there for some time. I was able to put myself in their situation and visualize how they must have felt at the time and now. What I heard them feel was what I felt too, which was the feeling of being let down, surprised and in shock. Through the looping process, I was able to give feedback, as to why the situation may have occured.

  • How did did you find the loving kindness meditation?

    • I found it good, as I was able to focus on how the other person deals with some similar situations, in the same way that I would. I felt that this person was free from most of the pain and suffering at the moment after having serious health problems last year.
